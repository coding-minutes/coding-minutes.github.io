About Campus Captain Program

At Coding Minutes, we call our Campus Ambassadors as Campus Captains. Our captains are highly skilled, motivated individuals and leaders in their own campus. If you think you are someone who is curious to learn new skills, make new friends, spread knowledge and believes in getting things done, then this program is just perfect for you.

Perks of a Campus Captain

Some of the benefits that you will enjoy exclusively as Campus Captain!

Course Giveaways

Based upon performance, Campus Captains will be eligible for free online courses & LIVE mentoring in coding gyms.

Networking

Leadership

Application Timeline

Apply & Get Shortlisted for Campus Captain Program in 2 simple steps.

1. Apply

Fill our your details in the Google Form attached below and wait for ~1 week for us to review your application.

2. Telephonic Interview

Once your application is accepted, we will schedule a phone call with you to learn about you and your experiences.

3. Get Offer Letter

Once we are convinced that you are good fit for this role, you will get your offer letter in about ~1-2 weeks and you can start working.
